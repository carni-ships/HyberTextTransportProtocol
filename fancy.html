How HyberText works

Step 01
📦

Pack & Compress

Your site is brotli-compressed and encoded with a 9-byte HYTE header.

Step 02
⛓️

Push as Calldata

One transaction to Berachain. The calldata is your website.

Step 03
🔑

Tx Hash = Address

The transaction hash is the permanent, immutable URL of your site.

Step 04
🌐

Resolve Anywhere

Any resolver fetches the tx, decodes HYTE, and renders your site.
